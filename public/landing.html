HOW IT WORKS

Plan smarter in 4 steps

CheapRoute uses real route data and your vehicle's specs to build the perfect fuel plan.

1

Enter your route

Type your start and end point. Add up to 10 stops. Google Maps calculates exact miles and drive time.

2

Select your vehicle

Pick year, make, and model. MPG and tank size auto-fill from the EPA database — no guessing.

3

Set your preferences

Choose your safety buffer, max detour time, and whether you want cheapest fuel or fastest trip.

4

Hit the road

See exactly where to stop, how much to buy, and navigate with Google Maps, Waze, or Apple Maps.
